Recharge and Withdrawal

1. There is no service charge for recharging.

2. The fee of withdrawal is divided into two parts. One part is the mining fee, which is collected when withdrawing to the digital currency account. The fee used for transferring on the blockchain, which will be dynamically adjusted according to the congestion of the blocknetwork and will fluctuate. The other part is the flow service fee. When there is unfinished flow in the account, a certain flow service fee shall be charged for withdrawal.


Spot

1. Spot transaction fee refers to the handling fee charged for the exchange of currency and quantity when you buy and sell in spot transaction.

2. The charge of spot transaction will be deducted from your total transaction amount. If BTC assets are obtained after the transaction, then BTC will be paid as the transaction handling fee.


Swap

1. Contract transaction fee refers to the handling fee you charge for opening or closing positions in the perpetual contract transaction.

2. The contract transaction fee is related to the quantity of contracts you trade, but not the market price. It’s needed to pay a certain USDT after the transaction as the contract transaction fee.
